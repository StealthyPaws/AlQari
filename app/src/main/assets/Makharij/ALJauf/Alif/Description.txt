ا (Alif Maddiyyah)

Makhrij (Articulation Point):
From the empty space in the mouth and throat, when preceded by Fatḥah.

How to Pronounce:
The sound flows openly without obstruction. The tone rises and stretches naturally. If preceded by a heavy (tafkhīm) letter, the Alif becomes heavy; if preceded by a light (tarqīq) letter, the Alif remains light.
Example: بَابٌ – طَالَ
