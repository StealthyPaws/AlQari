Al-Jawf refers to the empty space in the mouth and throat, it is a major area
and an articulation point at the same time. The three Madd (lengthened) letters originate from this general area, these letters are:
1- Waaw Sakinah preceded by a Dhammah
2- Yaa Sakinah preceded by a Kasrah
3- Alif preceded by a Fathah.

These three Madd letters do not have a specific place that they are pronounced from, unlike all the other letters. Their sound is produced from the point of
articulation of the previous letter. The Madd letters are prolonged two harakas if they are not followed by a hamzah or a sukoon.
