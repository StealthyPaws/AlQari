Al-Khashum refers to the nasal cavity, a large air-filled space above and behind the nose in the middle of the face. Each cavity is the continuation of one of the two nostrils. There is one articulation point in this area, used for the Ghunnah. Huroof pronounced with nasal resonance are called Al-Huroof Al-Khashumiyyah.
