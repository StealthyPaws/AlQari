ميم (Mīm)

Makhrij (Articulation Point):
From the meeting of the two lips (Ash-Shafatān). When pronouncing the میم part of the sound comes from the mouth whilst the other part comes from the nose.

How to Pronounce:
Both lips are completely closed, with part of the sound coming from the nasal cavity and part from the mouth, producing a soft, resonant sound.
