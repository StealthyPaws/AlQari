Ash-Shafatān refers to the lips, there are two points of articulation in the lips and each point produces one or more letters. Huroof pronounced using the lips are called Al-Ahuroof Ash-Shafatāniyyah.
1- Bottom lip touching the upper incisors: فاء (Fāʼ)
2- Meeting of the two lips: باء (Bāʼ) – ميم (Mīm) – واو (Wāw, non-Madd)

The bottom lip touching the upper incisors:
فاء (ف)
This letter is pronounced when the bottom lip touches the edges of the two upper front incisors.

The meeting of the two lips:
باء – ميم – واو
These letters are pronounced when the two lips come together. For ميم (Mīm), part of the sound also comes from the nasal cavity, while واو (Wāw) is articulated with lip rounding without complete closure
