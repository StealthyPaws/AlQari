لام (Lām)

Makhrij (Articulation Point):
From the front sides and tip of the tongue touching the gums of the incisors, canines, and premolars. This letter is pronounced from the front sides and the tip of the tongue touching what lies opposite to them, which are the gums of the two top front incisors, the two top lateral incisors, the two top canines, and the two top premolars. 
To pronounce the heavy Lam, the reciter should raise the back of the tongue towards the upper palate. These are called Huroof Tarafiyyah / Zalaqiyyah.

How to Pronounce:
The sound flows smoothly along the tongue, light in most cases, but heavy when preceded by certain letters (e.g., in “Allah”).
