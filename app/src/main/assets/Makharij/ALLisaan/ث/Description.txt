ثاء (Thā’)

Makhrij (Articulation Point):
These letters are pronounced from the tip of the tongue and the bottom edge of the front two upper incisors. The back of the tongue is raised towards the upper
palate to pronounce These are called Huroof Lithawiyyah.

How to Pronounce:
A soft, clear letter articulated by lightly pressing the tongue tip against the teeth edges.
