راء (Rā’)

Makhrij (Articulation Point):
This letter is pronounced from the tip of the tongue and the hard palate close to the gums of the front two upper incisors. These are called Huroof Tarafiyyah / Zalaqiyyah.

How to Pronounce:
There should be no trilling of the tongue when pronouncing this letter. One should physically feel the tongue hit the gums of the two upper front incisors, as the English (r) is pronounced without the tongue striking the gums of the front two upper incisors. Leaving a small space for the sound to run at the very tip of the tongue will help to pronounce the راء, correctly.
