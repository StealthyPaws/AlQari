نون (Nūn)

Makhrij (Articulation Point):
This letter is pronounced from the tip of the tongue and the gums of the front two upper incisors نون is pronounced slightly behind the gums (towards the upper palate) in comparison to the articulation point of
the لام Part of the sound comes from the mouth whilst the other part comes from the nose. These are called Huroof Tarafiyyah / Zalaqiyyah.

How to Pronounce:
The sound resonates partly in the mouth and partly in the nose, giving it a nasal quality.
