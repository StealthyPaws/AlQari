ظاء (Ẓā’)

Makhrij (Articulation Point):
These letters are pronounced from the tip of the tongue and the bottom edge of the front two upper incisors. The back of the tongue is raised towards the upper palate to pronounce ظاء. These are called Huroof Lithawiyyah.

How to Pronounce:
A heavy, emphatic sound, similar to English 'th' but thick and full.
